About Us

The Chengamanad Handloom Society is a dedicated group committed to preserving and promoting traditional handloom weaving techniques. Located in Chengamanad, Aluva, Kerala, our society is a small but vibrant community that plays a vital role in sustaining the local handloom industry. With 12 skilled women weavers, we create high-quality textiles that blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary designs.
